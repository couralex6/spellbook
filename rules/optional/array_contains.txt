Rule: replace array_contains(array, value) with contains(array, value) or contains_sequence(array, array[values])
Example: AND NOT ARRAY_CONTAINS(account_keys, "Vote111111111111111111111111111111111111111") -> AND NOT CONTAINS (account_keys, 'Vote111111111111111111111111111111111111111')
Example:  WHERE array_contains(account_keys, 'ATV2GHq2eCaHVbTrbMCP8MWATWnH8BzmsNMq5rXZ9DNC') -> WHERE CONTAINS (account_keys, 'ATV2GHq2eCaHVbTrbMCP8MWATWnH8BzmsNMq5rXZ9DNC')
